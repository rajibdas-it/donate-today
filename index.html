Corona Donation

When the wave of global pandemic- Coronavirus reached Bangladesh, more than hundreds of lives were lost from it. Besides, there were many who died with symptoms of Covid-19.

A special ‘Corona Fund’ was established to collect money for this purpose. You too can contribute in this fund to facilitate organizing funerals for the victims of this pandemic.

Prenatal Care for Low-Income Women

Every year, about 7,300 mothers die in the process of childbirth in Bangladesh. Lack of proper nutrition and medical facilities results in many mothers failing to give birth to healthy babies.

To support the pregnant women from underprivileged families, donate at Prenatal Care for Low-Income Women fund.

Medical Service

Medical needs for the underprivileged has always been a major concern for Quantum. Often, people living under the poverty line cannot afford medical bills and go untreated.

Hence We came forward to provide medical facilities to the deprived, underprivileged and insolvent families and individuals- for free.

Educational Scholarship

We believe that the poverty does not become an obstacle for the enlightenment of a meritorious student. You can donate in our Educational Scholarship fund.

Clothes Distribution

We formed the "Clothes Distribution Program" to distribute ordinary and winter clothing among the poor and needy across the country.
